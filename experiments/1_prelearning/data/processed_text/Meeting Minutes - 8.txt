F. ACTION ITEMS

Action, Information: 1. Resolution No. 2024/25-03, Order of Election, Request for Consolidation, and Specifications of
the Election Order

The Board discussed the current practice of providing up to $500 toward the costs associated with the printing and
distribution of a candidate's statement, and a possible increase to the amount.

Staff recommends Board approval of Resolution No. 2024/25-03, Order of Election, Request for Consolidation, and
Specifications of the Election Order, pursuant to the November 5, 2024 election for three governing board seats, and an
increase in the District's contribution to the candidate statements to a maximum of $800.

Motion by Kristin Speck, second by Gabi Blackman.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
Aye: Kristin Pelham, William Kuo, Dan Cherrier, Gabi Blackman, Kristin Speck
