G. SPECIAL ORDER OF BUSINESS - OPERATIONAL ITEMS/BOARD DISCUSSION AND/OR ACTION

Discussion, Information: 2. Ethnic Studies 1 Course Outline

Dr. Matt Campbell, Assistant Superintendent of Educational Services, introduced Sheri Sweeney, Director of Curriculum
and Instruction. Ms. Sweeney introduced Whitney Dwyer and Catie Tombs, and together they presented information on
the state’s Ethnic Studies course requirements and the work that DUSD’s Curriculum Council has been doing in designing
the district’s course offerings. Ms. Sweeney outlined the next steps in the adoption of curriculum and the course outline,
and the team addressed questions from the Board.

Public Comment:

An anonymous parent commented on the course being a one-semester course, rather than a year-long one; she also
questioned whether neighboring school districts have been consulted on the course development, and requested that
additional information be made available to parents and students.


Page 4:
Discussion, Presentation: 3. Budget Reduction Update #1

Superintendent Funk, along with Chris Hobbs, Assistant Superintendent of Business Services, presented information on
the Budget Town Hall meetings that they have held over the past few weeks. Also included was information on $11.5
million in potential reductions from which the Board may elect to focus to make the needed $5.5 million reduction. Each
of the potential reductions was explained by Superintendent Funk, and he noted staff’s recommendation to the Board to
focus on using Facilities funds generated by interest to make solar loan payments, eliminating the mid-shift custodial
positions, and pursuing the issue of furlough days with the bargaining units. Each of the trustees present discussed their
concerns, issues, and preferences for each of the possibilities presented, and Superintendent Funk and Assistant
Superintendent Hobbs addressed questions. Additional details were requested on several of the possibilities presented,
including the mid-shift custodial positions, TOSA positions, etc.

Public Comment:
An anonymous parent requested additional information on how to access the survey on the budget reduction possibilities,
and commented on some of the budget reduction areas being discussed, including class size, clerical reductions, etc.
